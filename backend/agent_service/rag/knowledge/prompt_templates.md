# Prompt 模板库 — 电商商品图优质 Prompt 模板

此文件包含各类图片类型的优质英文生图 prompt 模板，
适用于 Seedream / DALL-E 等主流生图模型。

---

## 白底主图 (Main Product Image)

### 标准白底主图
```
Professional e-commerce product photography of {product}.
Clean pure white background (RGB 255,255,255), studio lighting with soft diffused key light from front-left,
subtle fill light to eliminate harsh shadows.
Product centered and sharply focused, occupying 80-85% of frame.
High resolution, commercial photography quality.
No text, no logos, no watermarks, no reflections on surface.
```

### 悬浮效果主图
```
Floating product photography of {product}, suspended in mid-air against pure white background.
Soft shadow beneath product suggesting gentle elevation.
360-degree product visibility, sharp focus on product details and texture.
Clean commercial photography style, even lighting from all angles.
Minimalist composition, product as sole focal point.
```

### 亚马逊合规主图
```
Amazon-compliant product main image of {product}.
Pure white background (RGB 255,255,255) filling 100% of frame.
Product fills minimum 85% of image area.
Professional studio lighting, no shadows on background.
No props, no text, no watermarks, no inset images.
Crisp focus, true-to-life color representation.
```

## 场景图 (Lifestyle/Scene Images)

### 生活方式场景图
```
Lifestyle product photography of {product} in a modern, well-lit interior setting.
Natural window light from left side creating soft shadows.
Product placed on a minimalist surface with subtle complementary props (plants, textiles).
Shallow depth of field blurring background slightly, keeping product in sharp focus.
Warm and inviting atmosphere. Editorial photography style suitable for premium e-commerce.
```

### 户外自然光场景
```
Outdoor product photography of {product} in natural daylight.
Golden hour lighting with warm tones and soft directional shadows.
Natural background with blurred greenery or urban architecture.
Product as focal point in foreground, lifestyle usage context.
Aspirational yet authentic aesthetic. Candid-style composition.
```

### 桌面平铺场景 (Flat Lay)
```
Flat lay product photography of {product} viewed from directly above.
Arranged on a clean marble or wooden surface.
Complementary items placed in an aesthetically pleasing scattered composition around the product.
Soft overhead lighting eliminating harsh shadows.
Editorial minimal style with thoughtful negative space.
Popular on Instagram and lifestyle e-commerce platforms.
```

## 卖点标注图 (Feature Highlight)

### 功能标注图
```
E-commerce infographic-style product image highlighting key features of {product}.
Product on clean light gray background with visual callout lines pointing to specific features.
Clean modern design with subtle drop shadows.
Professional product photography combined with minimalist graphic design elements.
Ideal for product detail pages and Amazon A+ Content.
```

### 对比展示图
```
Split-screen comparison product image for {product}.
Left panel: before/problem state in muted tones showing the issue being solved.
Right panel: after/solution state with product in vibrant colors showing the benefit.
Clear arrow or transition indicator between panels.
Professional studio lighting on both sides.
Clean composition with balanced visual weight.
```

## 图标/缩略图 (Icon/Thumbnail)

### 电商图标
```
Square app icon style product image of {product}.
Minimalist flat design approach with clean geometric shapes.
Solid or gradient background in brand color.
Bold recognizable silhouette of product.
Suitable for e-commerce platform category icons and mobile thumbnails.
Center-aligned composition with generous padding.
```

## 细节/材质图 (Detail/Texture)

### 材质特写
```
Extreme close-up macro product photography of {product} showing fine material texture and details.
Shallow depth of field with selective focus on key detail area.
Soft directional lighting to reveal surface texture and material quality.
Premium commercial photography emphasizing craftsmanship.
Clean dark or neutral background to let details stand out.
```

### 结构分解图
```
Technical product photography of {product} showing construction and component layers.
Exploded view or cross-section style presentation.
Clean white or neutral gray background.
Precise lighting to reveal structural details without harsh shadows.
Labels or annotation lines indicating key components (can be added in post).
Suitable for product manuals and detail pages.
```

## 人物结合图 (Model/Person Images)

### 模特展示图
```
Professional fashion/lifestyle model photography showcasing {product} in use.
Natural, relaxed pose with genuine expression.
Model holding or interacting with product naturally.
Lifestyle setting appropriate for target demographic.
Soft natural lighting with professional fill.
Aspirational yet accessible aesthetic for e-commerce.
Clean composition with product clearly visible.
```

### 局部使用图
```
Close-up lifestyle product photography showing hands using {product}.
Focus on product interaction - hands shown in natural usage position.
Clean, well-groomed hands against neutral or lifestyle background.
Product as clear focal point with contextual usage being secondary.
Soft window lighting creating natural highlights on product surface.
Ideal for demonstrating product usage without full model photography.
```

```
E-commerce promotional product image of {product} in lifestyle setting.
```

## 特殊效果 Prompt 模板

### 玻璃/透明产品
```
Professional product photography of {product} on pure white background.
Specialized lighting technique for transparent/glass products:
backlight to show clarity and edge definition,
side lights with black cards to create clean dark edges,
top light for product surface details.
No distracting reflections. Commercial beverage/product photography style.
```

### 反光/金属产品
```
Professional product photography of {product} with metallic/shiny surface on graduated gray background.
Large softbox overhead creating smooth gradient reflection on product surface.
White and black reflector cards positioned to shape highlights and shadows.
Clean edge definition without blown highlights.
Premium commercial product photography showing material quality.
```

### 液体/化妆品
```
Professional beauty product photography of {product} on clean white background.
Soft diffused beauty lighting with ring light catchlight on product.
Subtle reflection on glossy surface below product.
Clean and elegant composition with luxury aesthetic.
Popular in beauty and cosmetics e-commerce.
Emphasize texture, color accuracy, and premium packaging.
```
